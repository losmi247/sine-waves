Sine Wave Animation

The following animation simulates a rightwards propagating sine wave whose equation is: $$y=A\sin(wt-kx)$$ Here \(A\) denotes the wave's amplitude, \(w\) its angular frequency (\(w=2 \pi f \), \(f\) being the ordinary frequency) and \(k\) its wavenumber (\(k=\frac{w}{u}\), \(u\) being the speed of the wave).
